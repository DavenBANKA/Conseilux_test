About Conseilux

Your trusted partner in English language assessment and training

Who We Are

Conseilux Training and Development is a leading provider of English language assessment and training solutions. We specialize in helping professionals, students, and organizations achieve their language learning goals through innovative testing methods and comprehensive training programs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accurate, accessible, and reliable English language assessments that help individuals understand their proficiency level and guide their learning journey. We believe that everyone deserves access to quality language evaluation tools.

Our Vision

We envision a world where language barriers no longer limit professional and personal growth. Through our testing platform and training programs, we aim to empower millions of learners worldwide to achieve their English language goals.

Why Choose Us

  • CEFR-aligned assessments validated by language experts
  • Instant results with detailed performance breakdown
  • Free, accessible testing for everyone
  • Comprehensive training programs for all levels
  • Global presence with local support
