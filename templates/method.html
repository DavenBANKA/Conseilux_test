Our Method

How we assess your English proficiency

CEFR-Aligned Assessment

Our test is carefully designed to align with the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR), the internationally recognized standard for language proficiency assessment.

Comprehensive Evaluation

The Conseilux Test evaluates multiple aspects of English language proficiency:

  • Grammar & Syntax: Understanding of English grammar rules and sentence structure
  • Vocabulary: Range and accuracy of word usage
  • Reading Comprehension: Ability to understand written English texts
  • Language Usage: Practical application of English in real-world contexts

Adaptive Testing

Our 90-question test adapts to your level, ensuring accurate assessment across all CEFR levels from A1 to C2. Each question is carefully selected to provide meaningful insights into your proficiency.

Instant Results

Upon completion, you receive immediate results including:

  • Your CEFR level (A1, A2, B1, B2, C1, or C2)
  • Overall score out of 90
  • Detailed breakdown by skill area
  • Downloadable PDF certificate

Validation & Accuracy

Our test has been validated against official CEFR assessments and maintains a 98% accuracy rate. We continuously update our question bank to ensure relevance and reliability.
