Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Conseilux Test

How long does the test take?

The test consists of 90 questions and typically takes 15-30 minutes to complete, depending on your pace. You can take breaks between sections if needed.

Is the test really free?

Yes! The Conseilux Test is completely free. You'll receive instant results and a downloadable certificate at no cost. There are no hidden fees or premium upgrades required.

Do I need to register or create an account?

No registration required! Simply start the test and receive your results immediately upon completion. We respect your privacy and don't require personal information.

Is the certificate recognized?

Our test is aligned with CEFR standards and widely recognized by employers and educational institutions worldwide. The certificate demonstrates your English proficiency level according to international standards.

Can I retake the test?

Absolutely! You can retake the test as many times as you want to track your progress and improvement. We recommend waiting at least a few weeks between attempts to allow time for learning.

What skills are tested?

The test evaluates grammar, vocabulary, reading comprehension, and overall language proficiency across all CEFR levels (A1 to C2). It covers practical English usage in real-world contexts.

How accurate is the test?

Our test maintains a 98% accuracy rate when compared to official CEFR assessments. It has been validated by language experts and continuously updated to ensure reliability.

Can I take the test on my phone?

Yes! The test is fully responsive and works on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and computers. You can take it anywhere with an internet connection.

What happens if I close the browser during the test?

Your progress will be lost if you close the browser or refresh the page. We recommend completing the test in one session, but you can take short breaks without closing the browser.

How do I download my certificate?

After completing the test, you'll see your results page with a "Download Certificate" button. Click it to generate and download your PDF certificate instantly.
